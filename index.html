Me as a professional

I graduated from Windesheim university of applied sciences, Zwolle in 2022 with a Bacholor in software engineering. Before that I finished a vocational education in the same field and I've been working as a mobile app developer for around five years now. I've worked with frameworks like React (Native) and Vue.js but I mostly develop iOS & Android apps in Flutter. I've done general backend programming in Python and PHP but I consider myself to be mostly a frontend developer.

Me as a musician

As a music lover with a perpetually growing vinyl collection I have always been interested in making music. My journey started when I picked up a guitar when I was little and ever since I haven't been able to put it down. With over ten years of experience teaching myself to play I've not only learned a lot but also broadened my horizon by taking up playing the piano(around 4 years) and as of late taking singing lessons. My musical influences range from Pantera to Paganini and there's so much I still want to learn in terms of music.

Aside from just playing I'm also taking part in online courses on the art of music production and I taught myself to build guitars from scratch. The result of which being the Stratocaster I'm holding. For those who are knowledgeable about lutherie I put in a Gibson '57 humbucker, Fender Texas Special single coils, 500k pots and a Vita-Q resistor to get as close to the tone of my musical heroes as possible.
