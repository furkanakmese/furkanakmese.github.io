About me

Hello. My name is Furkan Akmese and I'm a 21 year old software engineering student and employee from The Netherlands. This website contains my projects and general information about me.

Me as a software developer

I began my intermediate vocational education at Landstede, Raalte in 2015. Since then I became more and more interested in the world of IT and established myself as somewhat of an all around developer who loves making native mobile apps. I'm currently enrolled at the Windesheim university of Applied Sciences, with the goal of getting my degree in software engineering and to one day work in the United States.

Gripto

Gripto is a cryptocurrency platform meant to target both professional and casual cryptocurrency traders. I worked on the IOS/Android app, website and Api backend during my internship at Moor Interactive in Zwolle. Gripto is unfortunately not yet released, the website can be found here.

Rockgame

When I first got into making mobile apps I worked on a little music guessing game that combined my love for music with my new found interest of app development. At first this was an IOS exclusive app written in objective C, but later I made it cross platform with the Xamarin framework in C#. This Android app can be downloaded here.

Windesheim schedule app

Since Windesheim's official mobile app is not the best looking and working app, I decided to make a more intuitive and better looking app for checking the school schedule. The app is cross platform and made with React-Native in JavaScript, and available for download here (just for Android).

This website

While working at Moor Interactive in Zwolle I also learned to work with a content management system called Wagtail. Wagtail is the frontrunner for open source content management systems using the Django framework. After working with Django at Moor Interactive and attending the Wagtail Space event in Arnhem I decided to make my own personal website with Wagtail so it's easy to scale and customize.
